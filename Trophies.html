HISTORIC ACHIEVEMENTS

Five Consecutive Champions League Titles

The only club to win the European Cup/Champions League five times in a row (1956-1960), an unprecedented achievement in football history.

Three-Peat Champions (2016-2018)

First club to win three consecutive Champions League titles in the modern era, cementing their status as European kings.

FIFA Club of the 20th Century

Awarded by FIFA as the most successful club of the 1900s, recognizing decades of dominance and excellence.

Most Valuable Club Brand

Consistently ranked among the world's most valuable football clubs with a global fanbase exceeding 500 million supporters.

GOLDEN ERAS

1950s-1960s

The Di Stéfano Era

Dominated Europe with 6 European Cups in the competition's first 11 years. The legendary team featuring Di Stéfano, Puskás, and Gento.

1990s-2000s

The Galácticos

The era of superstars including Zidane, Ronaldo, Beckham, and Figo. Won 3 Champions League titles and established global dominance.

2010s-Present

Modern Dynasty

Unprecedented success with 6 Champions League titles in 10 years. The Cristiano Ronaldo era and continued excellence.
