Discover the Legacy

Step into the world of Real Madrid and explore the stadium that has witnessed countless historic moments. From the pitch where legends have played to the trophy room showcasing our 14 Champions League titles, the Santiago Bernabéu Tour offers an unforgettable experience for fans of all ages.

Trophy Room

Admire the collection of trophies, including our record 14 European Cups and numerous domestic honors.

Pitch Level

Walk along the sacred pitch and feel the atmosphere of match day from the players' perspective.

Players' Tunnel

Interactive Museum

Relive Real Madrid's history through multimedia exhibits and interactive displays.
